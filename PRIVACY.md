# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025-11-08

Dovecot.io is an independent, non-commercial diagnostic platform.  
We value your privacy and operate under a strict no-tracking principle.

---

## 1. Data Collection
We **do not** collect personal data, cookies, session identifiers, or analytics information.  
All checks (MX, SPF, DKIM, DMARC, TLS, etc.) are performed in real-time and results are not stored permanently.

---

## 2. Temporary Logs
Minimal logs may be retained for short periods (e.g., domain names tested and timestamps) to detect errors or abuse.  
These logs do **not** contain IP addresses or browser fingerprints.

---

## 3. Data Sharing
We do **not** sell, share, or exchange any diagnostic data with third parties.  
No external analytics or advertising platforms are used.

---

## 4. External Links
This site may contain links to third-party websites (such as GitHub).  
We are not responsible for their content or data handling practices.
